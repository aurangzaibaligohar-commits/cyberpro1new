Phishing Attacks

90%+ data breaches phishing se shuru hote hain. 2025 mein AI-powered phishing aur QR code attacks badh rahe hain.

Phishing Kya Hai?

Phishing ek social engineering attack hai jisme attacker legitimate entity ban kar users ko trick karta hai sensitive information (passwords, credit card details, bank login) share karne ke liye.

Yeh mostly email, SMS (smishing), phone call (vishing), fake websites ya QR codes ke through hota hai.

Latest Stat (2025): APWG ke mutabiq Q1 2025 mein 1 million+ phishing attacks record hue.

Common Types of Phishing

Email Phishing

Sabse common – fake bank, Microsoft, Amazon emails jo urgent action mangte hain.

Smishing (SMS Phishing)

Fake delivery alerts ya bank alerts SMS mein malicious links ke saath.

Vishing (Voice Phishing)

Phone call se impersonation – IRs, bank support, tech support.

Quishing (QR Code Phishing)

Fake QR codes parking tickets, menus ya posters mein – 2025 mein bohot rise.

Spear Phishing

Targeted attack – personal details use karke (employee, CEO impersonation).

AI-Powered Phishing

ChatGPT jaisi tech se perfect grammar aur personalized emails (2024-2025 trend).
