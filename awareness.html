Cyber Threats

Phishing

Deceptive emails or messages to trick you into revealing sensitive information.

Malware

Malicious software designed to damage or disable computers.

Ransomware

Encrypts your files and demands ransom for access.

Social Engineering

Manipulating people to divulge confidential information.

DDoS Attacks

Overwhelms websites or servers with traffic to make them unavailable.

Man-in-the-Middle

Intercepts communication between two parties to steal data.

Zero-Day Exploits

Attacks using unknown vulnerabilities before patches exist.

Credential Stuffing

Uses stolen username/password pairs to breach accounts.

Supply Chain Attacks

Compromising a vendor or software provider to attack many organizations at once.

AI-Powered Attacks

Using AI to create advanced phishing, deepfakes, or autonomous malware.

Deepfake Attacks

Fake videos or audio impersonating real people to trick victims.

Infostealers

Malware that steals saved passwords, cookies, crypto wallets, and more.
